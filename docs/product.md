# Product

## Problem

A small shop needs a simple way to manage products, stock changes, sales entries, and quick natural-language lookups without paying for a heavy ERP system.

## Users

- Admin: manages users, categories, inventory, and backups
- Staff: performs day-to-day inventory and sales work
- Viewer: optional read-only role for audit or reporting access

## Goals

- Fast daily usage on mobile screens
- Simple inventory CRUD
- Manual and linked sales entry
- Dashboard summaries and low-stock visibility
- Read-only LLM chat over approved data queries
- Easy low-cost deployment for a tiny internal team

## Non-goals for v1

- Billing and invoicing
- Barcode scanning
- Multi-shop support
- Product images
- LLM-triggered write actions
- Deep analytics or forecasting

## Success criteria

- Internal users can manage stock and sales from a phone browser
- Common inventory questions can be answered in plain English
- Changes are traceable enough for a small-team workflow
- The app remains understandable for Codex-driven implementation
